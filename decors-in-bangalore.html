Common Questions

FAQs on Decoration

Can you customize the theme?

Yes! Every event is unique. We help you pick colors, themes, and props that match your specific vision and budget.

Do you use real flowers?

We offer both premium artificial flowers (standard packages) and fresh real flowers (luxury packages).

How long does balloon decor last?

Our premium balloons typically last 1-2 days indoors. For outdoor events, weather conditions may affect longevity.

Setup time?

For standard birthdays, we need about 2-3 hours. For grand weddings, we may need 4-6 hours.
